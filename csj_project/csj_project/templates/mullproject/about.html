About

Fios

MUILE is based on research by Dr. Alasdair C. Whyte from Salen, Mull. It presents the place-names of the oldest recorded settlements in two medieval districts of south-east Mull: Forsa and Moloros. Forsa is the name which gives us the modern place-name Glen Forsa in the central part of Mull; the name Moloros is no longer used or known locally.

These place-names were first recorded in royal documents in the late 15th century but some of the names are much older than that. The names were created by Gaelic-speakers and by settlers speaking a language known as Old Norse which is closely related to modern Icelandic. These speakers of Old Norse were probably naming settlements in Mull from as early as the 9th century; some of the Gaelic place-names may have been established long before that.

Many of the place-names featured in MUILE are still used by local people today; others are long forgotten and are brought to life by MUILE.

These place-names give us an insight into life in Mull in the medieval period which no other source can give us, such as:

  • how the people of Mull perceived and understood their landscape;
  • how they used the land in rearing animals and growing crops;
  • the names of some local people;
  • local religion.

Tha MUILE stèidhte air rannsachadh leis an Dr. Alasdair C. MacIlleBhàin às an t-Sailean ann am Muile. Tha MUILE a’ taisbeanadh na h-ainmeanan- àite as sine a tha againn ann an dà sgìreachd ann an ceann an ear-dheas Mhuile sna meadhan-aoisean: Forsa agus Moloros. Tha an t-ainm Forsa aig muinntir an àite fhathast san ainm Gleann Forsa ann am meadhan an eilein; tha an t-ainm Moloros air a dhol a-mach à bith.

Chaidh na h-ainmeanan- àite seo a chlàradh an toiseach ann an cairtean rìoghail na h-Alba aig deireadh a’ chòigeamh linn deug ach tha cuid dhiubh fada nas sine na sin. Tha ainmeanan Gàidhlig agus ainmeanan Seann Lochlannais ann. Tha e an ìre mhath cinnteach gun deach cuid de na h-ainmeanan Lochlannais seo a stèidheachadh san 9mh linn; dh’fhaodadh cuid de na h-ainmeanan Gàidhlig a bhith fada nas sine na sin.

Tha tòrr de na h-ainmeanan seo aig muinntir an eilein fhathast ach tha cuid air a dhol a-mach à bith. Tha iad gan ath-bheòthachadh le MUILE.

Tha na h-ainmeanan a’ toirt fiosrachadh dhuinn mun eilean sna meadhan-aoisean nach fhaodadh tùs sam bith eile cur an cèill:
  • mar a chunnaic agus a thuig muinntir an eilein cruth na tìre;
  • mar a chuir muinntir an eilein am fearann gu feum a thaobh àiteachas;
  • ainmeanan daoine ionadach;
  • creideamh ionadach.
